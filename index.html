Once upon a time there was a group of shapes. They were mostly #FFFFFF and rectangular. They were mean and very serious.

And so, they kicked the 4 shapes that didn’t conform out of their gang, which was called the Right Angled Gang.

These shapes were very sad, but found great joy and comfort being around each other. They soon started having a lot of fun, started drinking and designing, reviewing movies and tv shows, and became the most well-known shape gang in the universe.

They even started making fun of squares and rectangles in what they like to call ‘Square Offs’!
